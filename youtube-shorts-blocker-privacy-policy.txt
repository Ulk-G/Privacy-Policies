Privacy Policy for The Youtube Shorts Blocker Google Chrome Extension

Effective Date: 13/04/2024

This Privacy Policy describes how The Youtube Shorts Blocker Google Chrome Extension handles user information.

Information Collection and Use
The Youtube Shorts Blocker Google Chrome Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. The app operates entirely offline and does not require any user registration or account creation.

The app does not collect any data, including but not limited to:
- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Device information (device ID, IP address, location, etc.)
- Usage data (app interactions, preferences, etc.)

The Youtube Shorts Blocker Google Chrome Extension is a standalone application that provides motivational quotes to users without any data collection or transmission.
